Successful customers
Adobe Generator (for Photoshop)

Generator is powered by Node.js, and provides I/O for Photoshop. You can access every bit of data in your psd, realtime, as you work, and let finally Photoshop fit into your workflow painlessly.

TinyMCE (for Ephox)

TinyMCE is a platform-independent web-based JavaScript/HTML WYSIWYG editor control. It’s designed to easily integrate with content management systems, including WordPress, Drupal, Joomla and Django.

BlueBank (for undisclosed customer)

BlueBank is an online banking solution prototyped in one month. The iOS app advises the user on day-to-day financial adversities by merging saving, budgeting and investing. All in one place.

Cancare (for undisclosed customer)

Cancare is an iOS app related to the world of the medical cannabis. Prototyped and developed over a three months timeframe, it focuses on both shopping and delivery aspect of the business.

rtail (Open Source Project)

rtail is a command line utility that grabs every line in stdin and broadcasts it over UDP. That's it. Nothing fancy. Nothing complicated. Tail log files, app output, or whatever you wish, using rtail broadcasting to an rtail-server. See multiple streams in the browser, in realtime.

Careers (for undisclosed customer)

Careers is a platform that connects HR and candidates. Recruiters can run granular, fine-tuned searches while the applicants can choose to disclose or not their sensible information.
